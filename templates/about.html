Meet the Team & Project

Project Overview

What is this System?

The Disease Prediction System is a Machine Learning–based web application that predicts possible diseases based on symptoms provided by the user. Built as a Final Year Project, this system helps users quickly identify potential health conditions using Artificial Intelligence — making basic healthcare guidance accessible to everyone.
